Health Benefits

Here's how Open Secret Chips can be beneficial to your health and why they are a great alternative.

Baked Chips

  • Baked chips typically contain less oil than fried chips, reducing overall fat content.
  • Fried chips are cooked in oil, absorbing more fat, which can lead to higher calorie intake.

Lower Trans Fats

  • Dehydrated fruits contain no added fats unless they are coated in sugar or oils (like banana chips).
  • Dehydrated vegetables are typically low in fat and calories, making them a healthy snack option.
